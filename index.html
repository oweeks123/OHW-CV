Introduction
Experienced clinician with 15 years in NHS and private sector, currently serving as Medical Director for Unscheduled Care at Cwm Taf Morgannwg UHB. Passionate about enhancing healthcare quality via smart, digital strategies. Skilled in strategic planning, innovation, and fostering collaborative multi-agency efforts for integrated care. Recognised for evidence-based decision-making and comprehensive expertise spanning primary care, secondary care, and diagnostic services
Change management
Demonstrated proficiency in change management within the NHS, serving as a clinical lead for the amalgamation of two urgent care services. Successfully engaged staff and the public, showcasing strong leadership and inspiring confidence in the incoming team. Additionally, led the introduction of 111 to urgent primary care, optimising departmental workflow.
Project management
Currently serving as the clinical lead for a health board's clinical navigation hub, focused on optimising patient pathways between primary and secondary care. Chaired pathway development and digital strategy workstreams, contributing to national benchmarking and establishing key performance indicators for enhanced healthcare delivery and patient outcomes
Clinical leadership
As a Medical Director, I provide strategic clinical leadership for emergency medicine, major trauma, urgent care, and acute medicine across three acute sites, catering to a population of 500,000. I manage senior line responsibilities and budgets in unscheduled care, reporting directly to the executive medical director.
Digital leadership
Experienced in rapid national digital health solutions, including leading the development of 111 Wales/Urgent Primary Care continuity solution. Proficient in Python, with basic SQL/HTML skills and familiarity with FHIR/openEHR data standards.
Vision for Healthcare
My vision for the future of healthcare entails an integrated care framework enabling seamless transitions among primary, secondary, and community care. Embracing a data-driven strategy with robust modeling ensures both value and efficiency. Data interoperability and system integration are pivotal for digital transformation, supported by strong organisational values. As a compassionate leader, I inspire collaboration for an enhanced and sustainable health service.
