Dr Owen Huw Weeks

MBBCh, MRCGP, MRCS, DO-HNS, DipRCPath

Languages: English and Welsh (fluent)

Croesty Farm, Coychurch Road, Pencoed, Wales, CF35 5LY · 07590 280594 · owen.weeks@wales.nhs.uk

I am a clinician with 14 years experience in the NHS and private sector. My main areas of interest are strategic planning and innovation with a particular emphasis on remote consultation and collaborative multi-disciplinary working. I am passionate about improving quality of healthcare both from a clinician and patient perspective by developing smart, efficient working strategies and utilising the latest in point of care testing, remote patient monitoring and machine learning. I am regularly praised for my inclusive leadership approach. I make a concious effort to meet with colleagues and stakeholders to demonstrate my approachability and willingness to listen. My approach to decision making is calculated and evidence based. By utilising all the available data to guide strategy I ensure that strategic plans provide value. In addition to clinical skills in primary care and emergency medicine, I am proficient in computer programming in Python and have basic HTML skills. I am well versed in the main GP patient management systems, Vision, EMIS, Adastra, Heydoc.
